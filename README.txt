数值分析中，龙格-库塔法（Runge-Kutta methods）是用于非线性常微分方程的解的重要的一类隐式或显式迭代法。这些技术由数学家卡尔·龙格和马丁·威尔海姆·库塔于1900年左右发明。
本项目主要用八阶显示龙格-库塔法来通过数值解研究常微分方程，具体实现是通过租云服务器完成计算。
目前正在研究4阶等质量天体在x-y平面上，在具有一定初始条件下的碰撞情况。

In numerical analysis, Runge-Kutta methods is an important implicit or explicit iterative method for solving nonlinear ordinary differential equations. These techniques were invented around 1900 by mathematicians Karl Runge and Martin Wilheim Guetta.
This project mainly uses the eight-order display Runge-Kutta methods to study ordinary differential equations through numerical solutions, and the specific realization is to complete the calculation by renting a cloud server.
The collision of fourth-order equal-mass bodies in the x-y plane under certain initial conditions is being studied.
